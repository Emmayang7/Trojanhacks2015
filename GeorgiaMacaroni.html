Georgia Macaroni

Cook Time:

15 minutes

Ingredients needed:

2 cups elbow macaroni
1 tablespoon butter
6-8 slices American cheese
1/4 cup evaporated milk
salt and pepper

How To Cook:

Step1:Boil 4 cups of salted water and add the macaroni; cook 8 minutes, or until done; Drain macaroni and set aside.

Step2:In a medium saucepan, melt butter over LOW heat.

Step3:Add cheese slices, one by one, until all are melted through, and cheese is smooth.

Step4:Stir in the canned milk, more or less to your desired consistency.

Step5:Add drained macaroni to cheese mixture; add salt and pepper to taste, and mix well until coated.

Serve immediately.
