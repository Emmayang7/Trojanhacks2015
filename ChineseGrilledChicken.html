Chinese Grilled Chicken

Cook Time:

25mins

Ingredients needed:

1/4 cup lemon Juice
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 chopped garlic clove
2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon honey
5 tablespoons soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger 2 boneless skinless chicken breast

How To Cook:

Step1:Combine all ingredients except chicken in a plastic bag.

Step3:Place in fridge for at least a few hours.

Step4:Remove chicken from marinade and grill until done turning once.
