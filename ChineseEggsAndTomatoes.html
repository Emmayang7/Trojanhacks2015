Chinese Eggs and Tomatoes

Cook Time:

10 minutes

Ingredients needed:

3 large eggs
1 tomato
some green onion
1 teaspoon oil
some salt
1 teaspoon sugar

How To Cook:

Step 1: Scramble the eggs in a non-stick pan, keeping them fairly chunky. When eggs are just about done, remove from the skillet and set aside.

Step 2: Add the oil to the skillet, and cook the tomatoes for a few minutes.

Step 3: Add salt and sugar to the tomatoes.

Step 4: Put the eggs back in the skillet, and mix everything together.
